King Kamehameha 1

Kamehameha I, also known as Kamehameha the Great, was the founder and first ruler of the Kingdom of Hawaii.

The details and summary element

Birth and Childhood

Kamehameha (known as Paiea at birth), was born to Keku'iapoiwa II, the niece of Alapainui, the upsurping ruler of Hawaii Island who had killed the two legitamate heirs of Keawe'ikekahiali'iokamoku during the civil war.
